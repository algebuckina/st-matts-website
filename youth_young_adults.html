Youth and Young Adults

Fusion

Fusion is our early schooled youth group from years 3 to 8 that meets fortnightly on Friday night of the school term starting in week 1, starting at 5pm and finishing at 7pm at St Matthew’s Uniting Church. Bring your children along for games and fellowship in a welcoming and safe environment.




Thrive

Thrive is a group for young adults Year 11 and up, that meets once a month for a social gathering and once a fortnight for a Bible Study. It aims to provide a safe space for fellowship and connection.
