About Us

We are a welcoming, friendly Christian community that proclaims and bears witness to the love of Jesus in service to the local and wider community. Located near the main centre of Baulkham Hills – we are well known for our preschool and our Op-shop: You are welcome to browse through this website below. You will find something for every age group from toddlers to seniors - as well as for men and women. We would be delighted to see you at St Matthew’s Baulkham Hills!
