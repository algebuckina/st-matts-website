Minister of the Word

Jon Humphries is a Uniting Church minister based in Australia. He has been known for his active role in various ministries within the Uniting Church, including his involvement in youth and community services, as well as his focus on social justice issues.

He often engages with the church community through thoughtful discussions on theology, contemporary issues, and spirituality, aiming to make Christianity more accessible and relevant to modern society. Jon Humphries is also recognized for his work in creating programs that address practical and spiritual needs, emphasizing inclusivity and justice.






Pastoral Carer

Sara Wienand is the pastoral care worker at St Matthews Uniting Church. She is a regular member of the 9:30 congregation, which she attends with her husband and two children. In her free time, she can usually be found with a book.

Sara works Sunday morning, Monday and Tuesday. Please call the office or stop by the church if you would like to get into contact with her.
