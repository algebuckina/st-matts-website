Netball Club

The St Matthew's Uniting Church Netball Club celebrated 44 years of running in 2021. We are one of the clubs that form Gooden Reserve Netball Association which is a smaller competition focused on fun, fairness, and encorages a family friendly environment.

The competition runs from March to August each year with games played every Saturday at Gooden Reserve in Baulkham Hills 12 to 4:30pm and training is held during the week.

Online registration for each season begins in December and closes early February. For general enquires or registering to play, please email the club at stmatthewsnetballclub@hotmail.com


Devisions (grades) are based on year group, not skill, and they are as follows:


Fun Net | Children in Kingergarten and Year 1

A fundermental and skill based program prior to joining a team once Children hit Year 2 of school.


Sub Juniors | Children in Years 2 and 3


Juniors | Girls in Years 4 and 5


Intermediate | Girls in Years 6 and 7


YPs | Girls in Years 8 to 12


Opens | Women who are Post Highschool / 18 years and older



Soccer Club

The St Matthews Soccer Club started in 1977 and since then has provided the game of soccer to boy's and girls of all ages within the core atmosphere of good fellowship. Over recent years our junior club has dropped but we are still here to provide the game of soccer within the church family and beyond.

We are always looking to welcome new members to the club, whether that be players or admin/support. If you are interested or have any questions please contact Jeff Cuff 0466825190

Yearly Overview

• 2019 was the last full season played and was highly successful with the highlights noted below.
• The 2020 season was interrupted by COVID which saw numbers fall during the season as players, however a dominant win was recorded in the State Cup.
• 2021 saw significant player movement and injuries deplete numbers, resulting in St Matthews forming a combined Premier League squad with Greystanes Churches. The competition was abandoned in June due to the COVID lockdown.

2019 Highlights

• Division 1 First Grade Champions
• Division 1 Reserve Grade Champions
• State Cup Runners Up
• A Grade Six a side Champions
• 3 players represented NSWCFA at the CFA National Titles in QLD. Rikard Bakke won the Golden Boot for the tournament. Rikard Bakke and David Cuff named in the Nation All Star team of the tournament.
• David Cuff played his 900th game for the club.
