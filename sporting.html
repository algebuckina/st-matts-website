Netball Club

The St Matthew's Uniting Church Netball Club celebrated 44 years of running in 2021. We are one of the clubs that form Gooden Reserve Netball Association which is a smaller competition focused on fun, fairness, and encorages a family friendly environment.

The competition runs from March to August each year with games played every Saturday at Gooden Reserve in Baulkham Hills 12 to 4:30pm and training is held during the week.

Online registration for each season begins in December and closes early February. For general enquires or registering to play, please email the club at stmatthewsnetballclub@hotmail.com


Devisions (grades) are based on year group, not skill, and they are as follows:


Fun Net | Children in Kingergarten and Year 1

A fundermental and skill based program prior to joining a team once Children hit Year 2 of school.


Sub Juniors | Children in Years 2 and 3


Juniors | Girls in Years 4 and 5


Intermediate | Girls in Years 6 and 7


YPs | Girls in Years 8 to 12


Opens | Women who are Post Highschool / 18 years and older
