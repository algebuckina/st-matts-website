Children and Families

Our Families with Children Ministry exists to support the role of the family in helping young people learn about God and encourage those facing the challenge of raising a young family in our Church.




KUCA

KUCA, our sunday school program runs at our 9:30am service throughout the school term, and with the work of our teachers, the message of Jesus and the Gospel is presented in an engaging and exciting way to our young children.




Fusion

Fusion is our early schooled youth group from years 3 to 8 that meets fortnitly on Friday night of the school term starting in week 1, starting at 5pm and finishing at 7pm at St Matthew’s Uniting Church.
